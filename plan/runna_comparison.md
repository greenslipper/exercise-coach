## Coaching Notes on Runna Plan

**What's better:**
- Much higher long run volume (16 km peak vs 8 km) — good aerobic base building
- Structured long runs with race pace sections (Wks 3–4) — great specific preparation
- The Boulder pyramid is an excellent confidence session
- The Athens 10×400m develops genuine speed
- The Nanjing reps at 3:14/km build VO2max headroom above race pace

**Cautions:**
- Week 5 is 37 km — significantly more than Freddie's current volume. Calf situation needs to be stable before that load
- 10×400m@2:53 (The Athens) is very fast — risk of pushing too hard if calves aren't settled
- Race week 9.9 km taper run is more than current plan — suits athletes with higher base but may be conservative enough given the 8×90s format
